E-COMMERCE STORE

ROLE/ DEVELOPER ■  CONTEXT/ COURSE PROJECT ■  PLATFORM/ DESKTOP&MOBILE ■  DATE/ AUGUST 2017
HTML   CSS   PHOTOSHOP   WORDPRESS  

This was my course project and also my-still on going-side project with objectives to create an responsive e-commerce website across multiple platforms.The client already run her bussiness through instagram and facebook, she plans to enlarge her selling range by creating an online website. This website build using a child theming practice of Storefront and using one of WordPress plugins, Woocommerce for its based.

 

THE PROCESS

CONCEPT:   The client already has a logo and pictures of her products, she requested a clean and simple website. Using brown tone colours will match the leather materials that she used for the products.

PREPARATION:   I designed a bold header to grab attention of the customers. For the sliding pictures, product pictures, etc I prepare them in Photoshop. Aside from the colour, to united the theme, I used sew or stitches graphic for some icons and buttons

PRODUCTION:   Here I am trying to reconstructed the design trough child theming from Storefront theme of WordPress. Also adding some JavaScript plugin for the main sliding images
