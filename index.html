In my journey since graduating from visual communication design with a Bachelor of Art degree, I have accomplished multiple positions at on air promotion departments in television, I have developed a passion in digital media. This intrigued my curiousity to further my knowledge in front end web development which has enriched my experience within the digital world.

The technical and logical aspects of front end web development compliment and deepen my love for visual communication design.

This website is to showcase my portfolio as a constant learning front end web developer, competent in visual communication design.
